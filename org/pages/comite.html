The BitBurger Works Council has been formally established to represent all employees of the company. Its current composition reflects a proportional distribution among the different departments and functional areas, ensuring equitable representation.

Constitution and Composition of the Committee

The main leadership structure is as follows:

  • President: Mr. Andrew Calveche
  • Secretary: Mr. Alvaro López
  • Member: Mr. Arturo Marco
  • Member: Ms. Lucía Fernández
  • Member: Ms. Ana Belmonte

These 5 representatives act on behalf of the diverse functional areas of the company, ensuring the advocacy of interests for the entire workforce. The commissions involving these employee representatives are:

Committee Commissions

Health and Safety Commission

Responsible: Andrew Calveche

Objectives:

  • Ensure safe and healthy working conditions for all staff.
  • Oversee compliance with current occupational risk prevention regulations.
  • Encourage worker participation in improving safety and health conditions.

Equality Commission

Responsible: Lucía Fernández

Objectives:

  • Promote and ensure equal treatment and opportunities for women and men in all areas of the company.
  • Eliminate any form of direct or indirect discrimination based on sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, or any other personal or social condition.
  • Ensure the correct implementation, monitoring, and evaluation of the company's Equality Plan.
  • Foster a work environment free from sexual and gender-based harassment.

Training Commission

Responsible: Arturo Marco and Ana Belmonte

Objectives:

  • Foster the professional and personal development of staff through continuous training.
  • Ensure that the training offered is relevant, of high quality, and responds to both the company's needs and the workers' aspirations.
  • Promote employee adaptability to technological and organizational changes, improving their employability.
  • Ensure equal access to training opportunities for all groups within the company.

Economic and Budgetary Control Commission

Responsible: Alvaro López

Objectives:

  • Ensure transparency in the company's economic and financial information that affects workers' interests.
  • Supervise the correct application of agreed economic accords and budgetary allocations for labor and social aspects.
  • Analyze the company's economic situation and its impact on employment and working conditions.
