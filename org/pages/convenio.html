BitBurger Collective Agreement

Improvements the Applicable Hotel and Restaurant Collective Agreement

Functional scope

This Collective Agreement regulates the labor relations between companies engaged in the hospitality, restaurant, and catering activities, including fast food establishments like BitBurger, and the workers who provide their services there.

Workday

The maximum workday will be 40 hours per week of effective work on average over the year. A minimum weekly rest period of a day and a half uninterrupted is established.

Vacation

All workers will enjoy a paid annual vacation period of 30 calendar business days.

Base Salary and Allowances

The base salary may be supplemented, where applicable, with bonuses for night shifts, holidays, and seniority.

Offenses and sanctions

Three types of offenses are established: minor, serious, and very serious. The corresponding sanctions may range from a verbal or written warning to disciplinary dismissal, depending on the severity and recurrence of the offense.
